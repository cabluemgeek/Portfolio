## About Me
 
Hello! I'm Kamelia, a graduate student bridging statistics, finance, and data science — currently pursuing a Master's in Financial Instruments Management (GIF) at CY Cergy Paris Université, after a Master's in Statistics and Economic Forecasting.

## 📂 Projects
 
### 🏦 [Bank Customer Churn Analysis](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/Churn-analysis-details.html)
Classification (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) + survival analysis (Cox Proportional Hazards) to predict *which* customers will churn and *when* in their lifecycle risk peaks. Explained with SHAP.
**Stack:** Python, XGBoost, SHAP, lifelines, Plotly, Streamlit
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/cabluemgeek/Customers-Churn-Analysis) · [Dashboard](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/Customers-Churn-Analysis/interactive-dash.html) · [Live predictor](https://customers-churn-analysis-5ffpxtupaphbftbmmpnu2y.streamlit.app/)
 
### 💳 [Credit Risk Scoring](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/Credit-risk-details.html)
Same dual approach applied to the Home Credit Default Risk dataset (~300,000 customers): three models compared, SHAP explainability, Cox hazard ratios for time-to-default.
**Stack:** Python, LightGBM, SHAP, joblib, optbinning, lifelines, Plotly, Streamlit
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/cabluemgeek/Credit-Risk-Scoring) · [Dashboard](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/Credit-Risk-Scoring/Interactive-dash.html) · [Live predictor](https://credit-risk-scoring-cpfthnr9nzx94dzgbyxn29.streamlit.app/)
 
### 🛢️ [Crude Oil Price Prediction](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/Crude-oil-details.html)
Forecasting Saharan Blend prices (2000–2022) — 7 ML/DL models (SVR, Random Forest, XGBoost, LSTM, CNN, and two hybrids) benchmarked across daily and monthly granularities, factoring in geopolitical risk (GPR index) and USD/DZD exchange rate.
**Stack:** Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, TensorFlow/Keras, Plotly
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/cabluemgeek/crude-oil-price-forecast) · [Dashboard](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/crude-oil-price-prediction/model_performance_comparison.html)
 
### 💬 [Multilingual Sentiment Analysis](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/feedback-nlp-details.html)
Sentiment and aspect classification on Yassir app reviews, written in French, English, and Algerian Arabic (darija) — including DziriBERT, a transformer pretrained on Algerian dialect.
**Stack:** Python, Transformers (mBERT, DziriBERT, XLM-RoBERTa, MARBERT), Scikit-learn, Streamlit, Hugging Face Hub
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/cabluemgeek/AI-Powered-Sentiment-Analysis-of-Customer-Feedback) · [Dashboard](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/AI-Powered-Sentiment-Analysis-of-Customer-Feedback/interactive-dashboard.html) · [Live classifier](https://ai-powered-sentiment-analysis-of-customer-feedback-baouusrlueu.streamlit.app/)
 
### 🚗 [Ride-Hailing Fee Prediction](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/trip-price-details.html)
End-to-end analysis of an Algerian ride-hailing dataset: rider/driver behavior patterns, geographic distribution across top Algerian cities, and a gradient-boosted fee prediction model with an interactive Folium map.
**Stack:** Python, Pandas, Plotly, LightGBM, CatBoost, Scikit-learn, Folium
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/cabluemgeek/ride-hailing-fee-prediction) · [Dashboard](https://cabluemgeek.github.io/ride-hailing-fee-prediction/ride_hailing_dashboard.html)
